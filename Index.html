Analysis of songs released in between 1922 and 2021

This project includes an analysis of songs released between 1922 and 2021..

The main analysis is related to the songs in terms of genre, music performance (danceability and energy), artists, demographics, and tracks released each year.

The data pulled from the Kaggle website and ETL process completed utilizing Python, Panda, and PostgreSQL. Visualization processed in Tableau. Also, ML has implemented for more detailed analysis.
